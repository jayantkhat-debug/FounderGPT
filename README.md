# 🚀 FounderGPT

FounderGPT is an AI-inspired startup planning platform that helps entrepreneurs turn ideas into business strategies.

## Features

### 💡 Startup Idea Generator
Generate startup concepts and opportunities.

### 💰 Business Model Generator
Create revenue streams and pricing strategies.

### 📈 Marketing Plan Generator
Build launch and growth strategies.

### 🎤 Investor Pitch Generator
Create investor-ready pitch summaries.

### 📄 Business Plan Generator
Generate a complete startup business plan.

## Built With

- Python
- Streamlit

## Project Goals

FounderGPT helps founders:

- Validate startup ideas
- Plan business models
- Develop marketing strategies
- Prepare investor pitches
- Create business plans
